About Me

Full-stack developer who enjoys building things that actually get used

I'm Cody Emami, a full-stack developer who enjoys building things that actually get used. I work with tools like React, Next.js, Node.js, SQL, and AWS, and I focus on creating apps that are clean, fast, and reliable from end to end.

Most of what I've learned comes from real projects, not tutorials.
